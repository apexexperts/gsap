What does Built Kindly do?

Built Kindly designs and builds digital products for healthcare and wellness brands. We focus on tools and experiences that are clear, usable, and built with care for the people they’re meant to help.
We work side by side with our partners from kickoff to launch, shaping both the product and the process with intention. We believe how something is made shows up in how it feels to use—so we bring kindness, curiosity, and a little creative boldness into every step.
